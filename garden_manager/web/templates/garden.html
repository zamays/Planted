What is Square-Foot Gardening?

Square-foot gardening is a method that divides your garden into 1-foot squares. Each square can hold a specific number of plants based on their size:

  • Large plants (tomatoes, peppers): 1 per square
  • Medium plants (lettuce, spinach): 4 per square
  • Small plants (radishes, carrots): 16 per square
